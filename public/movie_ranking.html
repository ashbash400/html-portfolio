My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl

Not a single shot or line of dialogue is wasted in this cinematic masterpiece that harkens back to '80s and '90s adventure movies while being wonderfully clever.

Pride & Prejudice (2005)

My go to comfort film. It is a warm, cozy, delightful hug.

The Mummy (1999)

"I was just looking for a good time!" and that's what this film is. It knows was it is and it excels at it while delivering on all childhood Ashley's curly haired, librarian dreams.
